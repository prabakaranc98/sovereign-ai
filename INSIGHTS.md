# Insights

> The distillery. Nothing enters this file directly — insights are earned in [SCRATCHPAD.md](SCRATCHPAD.md) and promoted here only when they clear the bar:
>
> 1. **Worth telling someone unprompted** — it would change a decision they'd make.
> 2. **Survives translation** — it can be retold legibly to an engineer, a domain scientist, a policymaker, a founder, and a beginner.
> 3. **Stress-tested** — the strongest contrarian version of it has been considered, not just the convenient version.
>
> Insights that keep growing here graduate into their own deep-dive folders.
